## Full Description *(max 4000 chars)*

IOU is the simplest way to remember the little things between friends.

You bought coffee. They covered dinner. They helped you move. You lent them your charger — again. These small favours are the fabric of real friendships, but they're easy to forget.

IOU gives you a quiet, private place to keep track.

── HOW IT WORKS ──

Add a friend using their email. Create an IOU — a favour owed or a favour given. Your friend sees it, accepts it, and when it's done, you both mark it complete. No awkwardness. No forgotten favours.

── THE LIVING TREE ──

Every friendship has a living tree — shared between you and your friend. It grows when you exchange favours, make wishes, and show up for each other. But it needs both of you. If one person stops participating, the tree starts to wither.

It's a quiet, living reflection of the effort you both put into the friendship.

── FEATURES ──

→ Friend system — connect with friends by email
→ IOUs — create, accept, decline, and complete favours with a clear history
→ Wishes — add wishes to your friendship and watch the tree grow
→ Categories — label each IOU (food, money, favour, and more)
→ Notifications — stay on top of new requests and updates
→ Themes — Morning Coffee (light) and Midnight Snuggle (dark)
→ Works on Android and web — pick up where you left off on any device

── PRIVATE BY DESIGN ──

Your IOUs and wishes are only visible to you and the friend they're shared with. No ads. No data selling. No social feed. Just you and your people.

── FREE TO USE ──

IOU is completely free. No subscriptions. No premium tier.

Little things, remembered.
