Little things, remembered.

IOU is a private space to track the little things between you and your closest friends.
Favours given, favours owed, wishes made — and a living tree that grows
when both of you show up for each other.

No social feed. No strangers. Just you and your people.

👋

Getting Started

Sign up & add your first friend

Sign up with your email or Google account. Set your display name and profile picture.

On the Home screen, tap + Friend and search by your friend's email. Once they accept — your friendship and your Living Tree begins.

🤝

IOUs — Track What You Owe

Creating an IOU

  1. Open a friend's screen → tap + New IOU
  2. Write what you owe (or what they owe you)
  3. Pick a category — Coffee, Food, Drinks, Favour, Money, or Other
  4. Add an optional note for context
  5. Tap Create IOU

Both people must agree it's complete. No shortcuts — that's what makes it honest.

💡 Tip: You earn +1 point every time an IOU you created gets completed.

The IOU lifecycle:

StatusWhat it means
📬 PendingWaiting for your friend to respond
✅ AcceptedBoth of you acknowledge it
🔔 Completion requestedCreator says "I did it"
✓ CompletedReceiver confirms it's done
💌

Wishes — Ask for What You Need

Making a wish

  1. Open a friend's screen → scroll to Wish
  2. Tap Plant a wish ✨
  3. Write your wish — be specific or keep it soft
  4. Pick a mood — Playful 🎉, Cozy 🤗, Sweet 🍯, Heartfelt 💝, Fun 🎮, or Calm 🌿
  5. Tap Send wish 💌
💡 Tip: Only one active wish per friendship at a time. Make it count.

What happens after you send a wish:

Your friend can Accept or say Not Right Now (with a kind note back). If accepted, they fulfil it in real life and tap Mark Done. You confirm it happened — and optionally write a thank you note.

🌟 Points: The friend who fulfils a wish earns +2 points.
🌳

The Living Tree

Your friendship, made visible

Every friendship has a living tree — shared between you and your friend. It reflects the effort you both put in over the last 14 days.

The tree is always visible on your friend's screen, and as a small emoji next to their name on the Home screen.

🌳

Growing

Both of you are showing up consistently

🌱

Dull

One of you has gone quiet — the tree is thirsty

💀

Dead

No activity from either side for 28+ days

🌿

Seedling

You've come back after a break — fresh start, history kept

⭐

Points — How They Work

Points are calculated over a rolling 14-day window — they update continuously, not monthly.

+1
Your IOU gets completed
+2
You fulfil a friend's wish

The tree stays alive when both of you contribute — and the less-active person earns at least half of what the more-active person earned. The threshold adapts to your natural rhythm.

📖 Example: You earned 6 points this fortnight. Your friend needs at least 3 to keep the tree healthy. If they've earned 4 — you're growing 🌳. If they've earned 1 — the tree goes dull 🌱.
💧

Keeping the Tree Alive

The tree is a quiet signal, not a score. Here's how to keep it healthy:

🤝Complete IOUs — both creating and confirming them counts
💌Make and fulfil wishes — wishes are worth double points
📅Stay consistent — one burst then silence will fade the tree
👫Both of you matter — one active person can't carry the tree alone

If your tree goes dull, you'll get a gentle notification. If it dies — don't worry. Start a new IOU or make a wish and it comes back as a seedling 🌿. All your history is kept.
